Flying Dollar Airport


Your gateway to the Poconos

Open dawn to dusk, the Flying Dollar Airport is a privately-owned, public-use airport adjacent to the famed Skytop Lodge. The oldest airport in the Poconos (formerly Barrett Airport, est.1927), the Flying Dollar is home to a well-maintained runway, marked by easy-to-see white cones, that is 2405' long and 100' wide on a heading of 02 - 20.
